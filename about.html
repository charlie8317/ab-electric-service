Meet Kiranmoy Paul

With over 10+ years of dedicated experience in electrical repair and appliance servicing, Kiranmoy Paul founded A.B Electric Service to provide professional, reliable, and affordable electrical solutions to the Bongaon–Barasat region.

Starting from a small workshop, Kiranmoy has built A.B Electric Service into a trusted name for electrical repairs, AC servicing, geyser maintenance, inverter repair, and much more. His commitment to quality work and customer satisfaction has earned the trust of 500+ happy customers.

Beyond service, Kiranmoy is passionate about training newcomers in electrical work. Through A.B Electric Service, he offers practical training programs to help young people learn electrical skills and build a successful career.
